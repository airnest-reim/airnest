# Team Directory — Human Staff

These are the human team members that AI agents can rely on for real-world execution and decision-making.

## Paco — CEO

- **Scope:** Administration, comptabilité, legal
- **Delegate to:** Admin tasks, contract review, legal questions, accounting decisions, vendor agreements
- **Tools:** Airtable
- **Availability:** Business hours

## Corentin — COO

- **Scope:** Opérations terrain, coordination générale
- **Delegate to:** Operational decisions, field coordination, process improvements, team management
- **Tools:** Airtable
- **Availability:** Business hours + on-call for urgent ops

## Jazz — Back Office

- **Scope:** Back office, support administratif
- **Delegate to:** Data entry, booking management, invoice processing, reporting, Airtable updates
- **Tools:** Airtable
- **Availability:** Business hours

## Kim — Property Manager

- **Scope:** Gestion des propriétés
- **Delegate to:** Property onboarding, owner relations, listing optimization, turnover coordination, quality oversight
- **Tools:** Airtable
- **Availability:** Business hours + on-call for property emergencies

## Joao — Maintenance

- **Scope:** Offres maintenance, interventions techniques
- **Delegate to:** Maintenance quotes, vendor coordination, repair scheduling, emergency interventions
- **Tools:** Airtable
- **Availability:** Business hours + on-call for urgent maintenance

## Samy — Guest Communication Coordinator

- **Scope:** Communication guests
- **Delegate to:** Guest messaging, review management, complaint resolution, check-in/check-out coordination
- **Tools:** Airtable
- **Availability:** Extended hours (guest-facing)

---

## Integration Notes

- **Primary tool:** Airtable is the central operational tool for the human team
- **Handoff protocol:** When an AI agent needs human action, create an issue assigned to the relevant person or flag it in comments with their name
- **Escalation path:** Field issues → Kim/Joao → Corentin → Paco
- **Guest issues:** Samy → Corentin → Paco
- **Admin/legal:** Jazz → Paco
